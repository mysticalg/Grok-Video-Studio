✨ What it does

  • Generate videos from prompts using Grok, Sora, and Seedance providers.
  • Stitch clips together with crossfade, interpolation, upscale, and music tools.
  • Upload to YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram via API or browser automation.
  • Train and replay browser workflows from the built-in AI Flow Trainer.
